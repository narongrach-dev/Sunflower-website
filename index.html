About Thai Massage & Spa

Thai Massage

Thai massage differs from Western styles by focusing on circulation and pressure points to promote internal health and relieve muscle tension. Known as Thai Yoga massage, it’s performed on a floor mattress, with the practitioner using hands, knees, legs, and feet to stretch and relieve pressure. The recipient wears loose, comfortable clothing, as no oils or products are used.

Foot Massage

Enjoy a soothing foot massage to unwind after a long day of travel. This traditional technique not only eases tension in the lower legs but also stimulates reflex points on the feet that correspond to major organs, promoting oxygen flow and overall wellness. Additional benefits include improved circulation, pain relief, and support in managing both acute and chronic conditions.

Oil Massage

Oil Massage involves the use of smooth, colorless, and odorless oils to enhance the massage experience. These oils help provide a more comfortable and relaxing treatment, promoting a soothing effect on the body. Unlike traditional Thai massage, which focuses on pressure without oil to address specific aches, Oil Massage also nourishes the skin, leaving it moisturized, healthy, and smooth after the session.

Aromatherapy Massage

A popular choice at Sunflower Thai Massage and Spa, this treatment combines the therapeutic power of essential oils derived from plants, flowers, and leaves with pressure point techniques. The oils are gently applied to the body using a Swedish massage technique to enhance relaxation. Ideal for unwinding after a long day of work or travel, this massage stimulates blood circulation and promotes lymphatic flow, leaving you refreshed and rejuvenated.

Back & Shoulder Massage

This focused massage uses precise finger pressure, along with hands and elbows, to target tension points in the back and shoulders. Performed on a specially designed massage chair, it provides maximum comfort while helping to relieve jet lag, reduce strain, loosen stiff joints, and improve circulation. It’s the perfect way to unwind after a long day at the office.

Four Hand Oil Massage

Indulge in our Four Hands Oil Massage, where two therapists use traditional Thai techniques combined with aromatic oils for a deeply relaxing experience. The massage includes gentle stretching, acupressure, and smooth oil application, performed on a mattress in loose clothing. It’s the perfect way to unwind, leaving you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.

Body Scrub

Our invigorating body scrub treatment gently exfoliates dead skin cells using a blend of sea salt, essential oils, water, and a choice of skin brushes or loofahs. Guests can select from six different scrubs, each tailored to suit their skin type. This treatment nourishes the skin, enhances circulation, and leaves your skin feeling cleansed, toned, and rejuvenated, even for the most sensitive skin.

Facial Treatment

Our facial treatment stimulates blood circulation, giving the face a youthful, bright appearance while reducing wrinkles. It helps remove dead skin cells, exfoliating and rejuvenating the skin. With immediate lifting and tightening effects, this non-invasive treatment includes cleansing, moisturizing, and other customized steps tailored to your specific skin type and needs, leaving your face refreshed, revitalized, and glowing.
